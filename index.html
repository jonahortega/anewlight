📅 Development Timeline

Week 1-2

Core Foundation

Essential app structure, user authentication, and basic event platform functionality.

Week 3-4

Data & Payment Integration

Critical infrastructure for Rutgers & USC data, Stripe/PayPal integration, and ticketing system.

Week 5-6

QR & Testing

QR code generation for tickets, validation system, and internal testing with sample events.

Week 7-8

Campus Launch

Live app deployment at both universities, real event creation, and initial user onboarding.

Week 9-10

Ambassador Program

Direct approach to Greek organizations, ambassador recruitment, and incentivized adoption.

Week 11-12

Enhanced Features

Google Maps integration, messaging system, and user experience improvements based on feedback.

Month 3-4

Scale & Optimize

Advanced analytics, role-based permissions, content moderation, and preparation for additional universities.

Month 4-5

National Expansion

Launch at Montclair State, Syracuse, University of Florida, Rowan, and Tulane with proven ambassador program.
